Privacy Policy — Conference Schedule

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Conference Schedule ("the app") is a free, unofficial companion app for browsing and planning a conference schedule; this build ships the SIGGRAPH 2026 schedule. This policy explains what personal information the app collects, why, and your choices. It is written to be read plainly, not as legalese.

Information we collect

When you first open the app, it asks for two pieces of information so you can start using it:

  • Your name
  • Your email address

That is the only personal information the app collects. The app does not collect your location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or device identifiers, and it contains no analytics or advertising software.

How we use it

  • To send you a one-time thank-you email confirming your sign-up.
  • To let the developer know, in aggregate, how many people have downloaded and started the app.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not build profiles about you.

How it is transmitted and stored

When you sign up, your name and email are sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to a Google Form. The responses are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the developer, and an automated Google Apps Script sends the notification and thank-you emails. Google acts as our data processor for these services; see Google's Privacy Policy.

On your device, the app stores only a small flag indicating that you have signed up (so it does not ask again). Your name and email are not stored on your device and are excluded from Android's automatic backup.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not share it with third parties except Google, which processes it solely to provide the form, spreadsheet, and email services described above.

Data retention and deletion

Your name and email are kept for as long as the app is maintained, so we can gauge interest and contact sign-ups if needed. You can ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing conferencescheduleapp@gmail.com. We will delete your entry from our records within 30 days.

Children

The app is intended for a general, professional conference audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

Information is transmitted using encryption in transit (HTTPS) and stored within Google's infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we limit what we collect to the minimum needed.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: conferencescheduleapp@gmail.com.
